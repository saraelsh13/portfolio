# Content systems designer for AI and SaaS products

I build the content infrastructure behind clear, trustworthy product knowledge.

My work spans UX content, technical documentation, information architecture, governance, quality evaluation, and AI-assisted workflows. I help teams turn complex and fragmented product information into systems that customers, Support Specialists, developers, and product teams can find, understand, and use with confidence.

## What I build

### Knowledge architecture for people and systems

I create task-based navigation, content models, taxonomies, source-of-truth rules, and metadata that make product knowledge easier to find, maintain, and reuse.

### Documentation as a product surface

I design technical, conceptual, procedural, API, and troubleshooting content around real user tasks. I use reusable patterns, versioned source files, and clear review processes to keep documentation reliable as products evolve.

### Content operations and governance

I establish standards, templates, ownership models, review cadences, and release-readiness checks that help teams create consistent, accurate content at scale.

### AI content quality and human oversight

I design retrieval-ready content, quality criteria, source traceability, escalation paths, and feedback loops that make AI-assisted experiences more dependable and easier to govern.

## How I work

```text
Discover → Model → Design → Build → Govern → Evaluate → Measure → Improve
```

I treat content as an end-to-end system. I connect user needs, product behaviour, content structure, documentation, governance, quality review, and measurable outcomes.
